Kerala

Trivandrum

Capital city, known for beaches and temples.

Kollam

Famous for Ashtamudi Lake and cashew industry.

Pathanamthitta

Gateway to Sabarimala, rich in pilgrimage tourism.

Kottayam

High literacy, home to publishing houses and rubber plantations.

Alappuzha

Known for backwaters, houseboats, and coir industry.

Idukki

Largest district by area, famous for hill stations and hydroelectric dams.

Ernamkulam

Commercial hub, includes Kochi city and major port.

Thrissur

Cultural capital, hosts the iconic Thrissur Pooram festival.

Palakkad

Known as Keralas rice bowl, rich in agriculture and heritage.

Malappuram

Most populous district, vibrant with education centers.

Kozhikkode

Historic trade center, birthplace of the Zamorin dynasty.

Wayanad

Scenic hill district, known for wildlife and tribal culture.

Kannur

Famous for Theyyam rituals and handloom industry.

Kazargod

Northernmost district, known for forts and linguistic diversity.
